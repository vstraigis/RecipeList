INSTRUCTIONS:

When you start an application you will be faced with the main menu, which consists of 3 choices:
1.View the recipes
2.Create a recipe
3.Inspiration

During every session you will be able to create your recipes by going into the "CreateRecipe" button.
There you will be able to name your recipe, add ingredients and add specific instructions or general
comments on how this dish should be cooked. Once you are done, press save and go back to the main menu!

When you have created at least one recipe, you can navigate to the "ViewRecipes" section. There you will
find a scrollable text area, where information will be displayed about all of your created recipes.

Lastly you have an option to inspire yourself by pressing the "Inspiration" button and enjoying the real 
state of the art!
